The Best Movies According to Jaikanesh

My top 3 movies of all-time

Thalapathy

"Thalapathy" (1991), directed by Mani Ratnam, is a celebrated Tamil movie starring Rajinikanth, Mammootty, and Shobana. It is a powerful film that blends mythological elements with contemporary issues, focusing on themes of friendship, loyalty, and justice.

Jailer

The story follows Muthuvel Pandian (Rajinikanth), a retired jailer who lives a peaceful life with his family. He is a caring father and a doting grandfather. His son, Arjun (Vasanth Ravi), is a law-abiding police officer tasked with investigating a notorious gang involved in stealing valuable temple artifacts. Arjun's investigation leads him to a dangerous gangster named Varman (Vinayakan), whose operation threatens the country’s cultural heritage.

When Arjun suddenly goes missing during his investigation, Muthuvel’s life is thrown into turmoil. Believing his son has been killed by Varman’s gang, the retired jailer decides to take justice into his own hands. This triggers a transformation in Muthuvel, as he taps into his past experience and former persona, a man capable of ruthless actions.

The story then shifts to a darker tone, where Muthuvel orchestrates a masterful revenge plan. Along the way, he confronts several key players in the criminal underworld, showing his cunning and strength. There are also plenty of action sequences where Rajinikanth's larger-than-life persona is highlighted, blending gritty combat with his signature swagger.

Annamalai

"Annamalai" is a story of friendship, betrayal, and redemption, set against the backdrop of socio-economic divides.
